ABOUT US

Online Movie Ticket Booking System is to book movies and foods etc.

Online Movie Ticket Booking System is a website to provide the customers facility to book tickets for a movie online and to gather information about the movies and theaters. Customer needs to register at the site to book tickets to the movie. After selecting the show, the user is presented a seating layout so that he can select seats of his choice. Thereafter he is redirected to the payment gateway for making a transaction. User can update his profile, take a print out of the ticket and also view his booking history.
